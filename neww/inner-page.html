Green Square Apartments at Dubai Hills Estate

More Than Your Home, Ideal Family Destination

Premium apartments where you can start, run, and grow your business.  Green Square Apartments at Dubai Hills Estate brings you a sophisticated balance of private apartment living and high-end community amenities. Green Square Residences is not just about improving upon the ‘work-live-play’ paradigm and  taking it to new heights. You can find the perfect work-life balance.

A contemporary social hub offering 1, 2, and 3 bedroom apartments along side Dubai Hills Park and Dubai Hills Mall. Your dream work-space is here. Emaar brings you premium apartments in a vibrant residential community alongside Dubai Hills Park and Dubai Hills Boulevard where you can set up, run and grow your business. Features elegantly designed and premium quality apartments, making it ideal for individuals, couples and families alike. Why Invest in Green Square Apartments?

Working from home is proven to afford you flexible working hours, fewer distractions, and an improved work-life balance. What’s more, your newly set-up business at Green Square residences gives you:

More About Green Square Off Plan Project at Dubai Hills Estate

Green Square building is fitted with fully-appointed modern gymnasiums and inviting shared spaces that facilitate a stronger sense of community. The perfect place to work, live, and play. Green Square combines the best of nature and urban living. Suited to both individuals and families, Green Square homes are equipped with all your essential comforts. The contemporary building embrace courtyards populated with Zen and yoga gardens, infinity pools, children’s play areas and running tracks to support you in nurturing an active lifestyle. While Dubai Hills Mall, just down the road, takes care of all your shopping and entertainment needs.

Dubai Hills Estate is centrally located within MBR City, positioned by the junction of Umm Al Suqeim Road and Al Khail Road. The location offers easy access to Sheikh Zayed Road and it is only a 10-minute drive from Downtown Dubai. Residents will also have access to the Etihad Rail and Dubai Metro lines which will border the development, enabling fast and easy connection.
